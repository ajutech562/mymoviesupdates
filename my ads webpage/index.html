🎬 Lokah – Chapter One: Chandra

🛡️ Overview First female-led superhero film in Malayalam cinema, launching the ambitious Lokah cinematic universe. Directed and written by Dominic Arun, and produced by Dulquer Salmaan under his production banner, Wayfarer Films.

📅 Production Timeline Pre-production began September 13, 2024. Filming started on September 12, 2024, and wrapped up by January 30, 2025, spanning 94 days .

🎭 Cast & Crew Stars: Kalyani Priyadarshan (in her first superhero role) and Naslen K. Gafoor in the lead. Creative Team: Cinematography: Nimish Ravi Editing: Chaman Chakko Music: Jakes Bejoy Action Choreography: Yannick Ben Production Design: Banglan Costume Design: Melwy J (with collaboration from Archana Akhil Rao) Executive Producers: Jom Varghese & Bibin Perumballi

✨ Story & Theme Plot (Tenative): Follows a young woman named Chandra who discovers extraordinary powers. She steps into a hero’s role to fight dark forces that threaten her world. The narrative blends myth, sci-fi, folklore, and a dystopian tone, offering a visually rich and emotionally driven storyline The Times of India FilmiBeat Filmfare. The teaser reveals scenes of high-stakes action, including Chandra confronting armed soldiers in a gritty dystopian setting

🎥 Cinematic Impact Designed to kick off the Lokah superhero universe, inspired by Kerala’s mythology and cultural storytelling traditions Bollywood Hungama twentyfournews.com . Director Dominic Arun has emphasized that the emergence of a female superhero was never a calculated hook, but a natural evolution of story and characters since 2020
